Introduction

Ever felt it hard to keep track of all those amazing tasks out there in the mulearn discord server, or ever felt it overwhelming to do tasks one by one, or ever felt it hard to find tasks across numerous Interest Groups!

mulearn Companion app simply is an amazing todo list app you must have while exploring the world of mulearn. With this you could easily find all those tasks in the server, just by searching for it as well as filter them by Interest Groups as well. Almost all the tasks are upto date with the discord server.

This page here explains you how to use this app effectively, effortlessly and meaningfully!

Initially this website is designed in such a way that you could use it like a todo app, just for managing your day-to-day mulearn tasks. Here you could find all tasks from mulearn discord server, schedule them so that you could complete them one by one, Mark tasks as completed so that you could keep track of all those completed tasks effectively. Also once you mark a task as completed, it's karma points is added to your profile and you could view it in the dashboard. The Dashboard

The dashboard page, is where our todo app is actually integrated. There the first thing you could see are three stats. Among them the first one shows how many tasks you have added to your tasks list. The next one shows how many tasks you have currently marked as completed, and the last one shows how many karma points you currently have (Total points of the tasks you have marked as completed).

Important Features and how to use them

Now let's see what all things you can do and see here.

In the Dashboard page, you could find 5 important options,

  • All mulearn Tasks: View all mulearn discord tasks
  • Find Task: Search for a task.
  • Your tasks List: All tasks you scheduled to complete.
  • Removed Tasks: Tasks you have once sheduled to complete, but later deschedules it.
  • Completed Tasks: Tasks you have marked as complete.

1. All mulearn Tasks

This option gives you the complete list of all available tasks in mulearn discord server. You could add the task to your tasks list by clicking on the icon. If you have already have this task in your task list, you'll have icon instead. If you have already marked this task as completed, you will have icon instead.

2. Find task

Among those dosens of tasks, if you want to find a task specifically using its hashtag or any keywords, you could search for it using this option.

3. Your Tasks List

Here you could view all the tasks you have scheduled to complete. After completing the task, you could click on the icon to mark that task as completed. Also you could click on the icon to remove (deschedule) that task from your tasks list.

4. Remove Tasks

After removing a task from your tasks list, if you want see it again, you could simply go to the removed tasks section and find all those removed tasks there and you could also track it again by clicking on the icon.

5. Completed Tasks

Here you could view all tasks you have marked as completed. Once you mark any task as completed, it is a permanent action and cannot be changed. There is no way to mark it as incomplete or add it to your tasks list once you marked it as complete. Also the karma points in the dashboard here is calculated based on completed tasks.

User data handling

As of now we does not save user data to the cloud, which means your data (task list, tracked tasks, completed tasks, points etc...) are saved in your device itself. Therefore, if you delete your browser data, all the data will be lost.

Step By Step Walkthrough

Basic Operations

  • Schedule your first task
  • View your scheduled tasks
  • Removing a task from your tasks list
  • View Descheduled Task
  • Mark a task as completed
  • View Completed tasks

In this Step By Step Walkthrough we are going to explore some basic features of this companion and how to use them.

Before starting please open dashboard in a new tab and follow instructions from this tab while doing it in the new tab.

After opening the dashboard, you could see your stats at the top just below the text Dashboard.

Schedule your first task

The first thing we are going to do is to schedule your first task. For that click on the All mulearn Tasks option. Now you could see a long list of tasks. There find any tasks of your interest and click on the (Task Track) icon there. Now you'll get a confirmation message, click Yes to track that task. Tracking a task will add that task to your tasks list.

Once you track a task, you could see the icon changed to icon. This icon represents that the task is already tracked. If you click on it a another message will pops up and if you click yes, the task will be removed from your tasks list.

View your scheduled tasks

After scheduling tasks you could view those tasks in your Tasks List. Click on the Your Tasks List option in the dashboard, and you could see all the tasks you have currently tracked. After completing a task, you could mark it as completed by clicking on the icon. If you want to remove that task from your tasks list, click on the icon.

Removing a task from your tasks list

After scheduling some tasks you may start descheduling some tasks. To remove a task from your tasks list, click on Your Tasks List option in the dashboard, and you could see all the tasks you have currently tracked. Now click on the icon of the task you want to deshedule/remove and you can see a confirmation message, clik Yes to remove that task from your tasks list.

View Descheduled Task

Once you deshedule a task, it is moved to the Removed tasks List. You can see all descheduled tasks from the Removed Tasks option in the dashboard. Click on the Removed Tasks option in the dashboard. You can see the list of all tasks you have desheduled. If you want to schedule it again you could click on the icon of that task there. Click on that icon and track a task again.

Mark a task as completed

After tracking a task, you could view it in You Tasks List. After completing it, you can mark that task as completed. Marking a task as completed is a permanent action and cannot undo it. It is a one time action for each tasks. To mark a task as completed, click on Your Tasks List option in the dashboard and you could see all your tasks. There click on the icon of a task and there will be a confirmation message, click on Yes to mark that task as completed. After that the task will be removed from your tasks list and you could see it in the Completed Tasks list.

View Completed tasks

To see all the completed tasks, click on the Completed Tasks option in the dashboard and you could see all those tasks you have marked as completed. Once you mark a task as completed, it cannot be marked as incomplete again.

Thats all for getting started, wish you a very best and feel free to explore further...
